Caesar Salad

  • Servings: 2

Ingredients:

Dressing:

  • 3 anchovy filets

  • 2 cloves garlic, finely chopped

  • 1/2 lemon, juiced

  • 2 tablespoons red wine vinegar

  • 1 large egg yolk

  • 1 tablespoon Dijon mustard

  • 1 dash Worcestershire sauce

  • 1/4 cup olive oil

  • salt and ground black pepper to taste

Salad:

  • 1/2 head romaine lettuce, chopped

  • 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese

  • 2 tablespoons croutons


Directions

Step 1:

Gather all the ingredients

Step 2:

To make the Caesar dressing: Mash anchovy fillets and garlic in a large salad bowl. Add lemon juice, red wine vinegar, Dijon mustard, egg yolk, and Worcestershire sauce; whisk until smooth and creamy. Gradually stream in olive oil while whisking constantly. Season to taste with salt and black pepper.

Step 3:

To serve: Gently mix romaine lettuce and Parmesan cheese into dressing until throughly coated.

Step 4:

Top Caesar salad with croutons and enjoy!

Cook's Note:

If you have trouble getting the anchovies to mash, add a couple of croutons. I find that the hard edges of the croutons cut into the anchovies.
