Garlic Butter Grilled Cheese Hot Dog

Click here to go home

Basic Information:

  • Prep Time: 5 minutes

  • Cook Time: 10 minutes

  • Total Time: 15 minutes

  • Servings: 8

Ingredients:

  • 8 hot dogs

  • 1/2 cup garlic butter, divided

  • 8 hot dog buns

  • 2 cups shredded cheese


Directions

Step 1:

Preheat the oven to 400 degrees F (200 degrees C). Score hot dogs, brush with garlic butter, and place on a baking sheet.

Step 2:

Bake hot dogs in the preheated oven until nicely browned, 6 to 8 minutes.

Step 3:

Meanwhile, brush insides of hot dog buns with garlic butter; toast buns in a large skillet over medium heat until golden brown. Set aside.

Step 4:

In the same skillet, lay down 1/4 cup cheese and place a bun face down on top of cheese. Cook undisturbed until cheese is melted. Remove from skillet and repeat with remaining buns.

Step 5:

Place hot dogs in prepared buns and serve immediately.
